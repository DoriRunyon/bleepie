Bleepie

Bleepies come from the planet Crombo, 3.2 zillion light years away from Earth.
They are great pets but require special care.

Every day, you will be asked for your blood alcohol level.
If the level is too high, your bleepie will become ill.

At very high levels, your bleepie will die. Don't let your bleepie die!


    For version 1.0, the BAL requests will be texted.
    Use of the app will require a breathalyzer and users will be able to see their BAL data over time.
    If a user's BAL is more than zero, the user may receive a second request asking for another BAL.
